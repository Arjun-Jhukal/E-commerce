Shipping

Chroma Apparel offers fast and reliable shipping to customers worldwide. Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days and shipped via a variety of carriers, including USPS, UPS, and FedEx.

Shipping rates are based on the weight and destination of the order, and customers can choose from a range of shipping options at checkout. We also offer free shipping on orders over a certain amount (varies by location).

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information so you can easily track your package.

Exchange

Chroma Apparel wants you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer a hassle-free exchange policy. Customers have up to 30 days from the date of delivery to exchange their item for a different size or style. To initiate an exchange, please contact our customer support team with your order number and details of the item you wish to exchange.

Please note that all items must be returned in new, unused condition with all original tags and packaging. Chroma Apparel reserves the right to refuse any exchanges that do not meet these criteria.

Customers are responsible for all shipping costs associated with the exchange, including shipping the original item back to us and the shipping cost for the new item. Once we receive the returned item, we will process the exchange and ship out the new item as soon as possible.

Conditions For Exchange

  1. The item must be returned in new, unused condition with all original tags and packaging intact.
  2. The item must be returned within 30 days from the date of delivery.
  3. The customer must contact Chroma Apparel's customer support team to initiate the exchange and provide their order number and details of the item they wish to exchange.
  4. The customer is responsible for all shipping costs associated with the exchange, including shipping the original item back to Chroma Apparel and the shipping cost for the new item.
  5. Chroma Apparel reserves the right to refuse any exchanges that do not meet the above conditions.
  6. Chroma Apparel will process the exchange as soon as possible after receiving the returned item, typically within 3-5 business days.
  7. If the requested exchange item is not available, Chroma Apparel will issue a refund for the original item.
  8. Chroma Apparel reserves the right to modify or cancel this exchange policy at any time, without notice.
